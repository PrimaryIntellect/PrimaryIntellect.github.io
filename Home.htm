Meet the First of Many:
Gaido

Gaido is a personal security and aid robot that will assist students or families all over the world with its diverse utilities. It has the ability to find different reliable sources whether it be for instructions or research papers and more. That's not all it can do though. It's also a security measure. It comes with a feature that will send you security alerts when an unrecognizable face is seen with its built-in camera. It also has a Bluetooth feature that allows phone use.
