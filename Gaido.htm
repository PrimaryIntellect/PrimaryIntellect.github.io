Gaido: A Best Friend For Students and Families



What is Gaido?

Gaido, meaning guide in Japanese, is a personal assistant for students of all grades, for a busy family, for people that like utility versatility, or for someone that wants a companion. Gaido is an absolute must-have if you fall into any of these categories. Have a research paper that needs to be done? Tell it what your topic is and it will send you links to the most credible websites pertaining to your research needs. Afraid your pet won't scare away any unwanted guests? It has a camera that will send an alert to you via text and email along with a picture. But wait, there's more! Gaido also supports Bluetooth and can be voice activated. It will connect to your phone and to your headphones. The headphones can be used to answer the phone or Skype calls with a connection to your phone with the official app even when it's off or lost. It has a speaker with an always warming voice that will also tell you about reminders that set on your phone and on your phone's calendar. It can roam around your house and also comes with a flashlight to “Gaido” you in the dark.
